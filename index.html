About Our Company

"Magic of Knowledge (MKnets)" is an Iraqi company founded in 2014 by a group of highly skilled contributors with cutting-edge experience in all aspects of the ICT industry.

MKnets has a data center and a cutting-edge training center where it delivers a wide range of courses covering a variety of specializations such as networks, information technology, languages, translation, management, and human resource development.

MKnets is a one-of-a-kind company that provides innovative IT solutions and business management because of a sophisticated network of alliances with globally recognized companies such as CISCO , HUAWEI , DELL, VEEAM,FORTINET , KASPERSKY, and SOPHOS.

Services we provide MKnets offers a diverse set of services, including IT solutions, business management, and training sessions that cover a wide range of topics, including engineering, networking, languages, and management.
